预定义管道(过滤器) pipe

原数据: Tom and jarry
1. lowercase: 转小写
{{"Tom and jarry" | lowercase }}
2. uppercase: 转大写
{{"Tom and jarry" | uppercase }}
3. titlecase: 首字母大写
{{"Tom and jarry" | titlecase }}
4. slice: start [: end] --> 截取
{{"Tom and jarry" | slice:2 }}
{{"Tom and jarry" | slice:2 :5 }} -->(2, 5)
5. json: 把js对象序列化为 json字符串
{{empList | json}}
6. number:整数最少5位, 点后最少1位, 最多3位: (5.1-3)
默认: {{empList.salary | number}}
(5.1-3) {{empList.salary | number: "5.1-3"}}
(4.2-2) {{empList.salary | number: "4.2-2"}}
7. currency:"¥" 数字转货币
{{empList.salary | currency}}
{{empList.salary | currency:"¥"}}
8. date: "yyyy-MM-dd hh:mm:ss" 日期
{{empList.birthday | date: "yyyy-MM-dd hh:mm:ss" }}
